Privacy Policy

Last updated July 21, 2026

Bonkey Cards does not automatically collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers of any kind. Bonkey Cards is an offline-first card game app — every game runs entirely on the device you're playing on. The only times anything leaves your device are when you choose to connect directly to another nearby device over Bluetooth to play together (only the game moves you make are shared, directly with that device — never with us or with anyone else), and when you choose to use the optional "Report a bug or idea" form to email us — and even then, only what you typed and reviewed is sent, and only once you tap Send yourself.

Who we are

Bonkey Cards is made by Bonkey Apps. If you have any questions about this policy or about the app, contact us at bonkey.apps@gmail.com.

Information we collect

None automatically. Specifically, Bonkey Cards does not, on its own:

  • Require or offer account creation, sign-in, or sign-up of any kind.
  • Collect names, email addresses, birthdates, or any other personal information.
  • Include any analytics software development kit (SDK).
  • Include any advertising SDK or show any ads.
  • Use cookies, device fingerprinting, advertising identifiers, or any other cross-app or cross-site tracking technology.
  • Transmit gameplay data or game state to any server. There is no server — Bonkey Cards works entirely offline.
  • Send anything off your device without your knowledge. The one exception is the optional "Report a bug or idea" form described below, which only sends what you explicitly reviewed and chose to send.

What stays on your device

Game settings and any local preferences are stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes this data. We have no access to it, cannot see it, and never receive a copy of it.

Bluetooth multiplayer

Bonkey Cards lets you play Hearts, Spades, Tens, Go Fish, and Gin Rummy with other people in the same room by connecting devices directly over Bluetooth — not through the internet and not through any Bonkey Apps server. This local, peer-to-peer connection is used only to exchange the structured game moves (for example, which card was played) between the devices a player has chosen to connect to. Nothing from a Bluetooth game is sent to Bonkey Apps or stored anywhere beyond the devices actually playing.

Bluetooth ("Nearby devices") permission is requested solely to discover and connect to nearby devices for local multiplayer. It is never used to determine or share your location, and no data collected through it is sent to Bonkey Apps or any third party.

Solo play against the built-in AI opponent never uses Bluetooth or any network connection at all — the AI runs entirely on your device using the exact same rules engine as multiplayer.

Bug or feature reports

Settings → About includes an optional "Report a bug or idea" form. If you choose to use it, Bonkey Cards opens a pre-filled draft in your own email app (or a mailto: link on the web version), addressed to bonkey.apps@gmail.com — the shared contact address for every Bonkey app. The app itself never sends this email; you review the draft in your own mail app and decide whether to send it, edit it, or cancel, exactly like composing any other email.

The draft always includes whatever you typed. It can optionally include a short debug-info block — shown to you before you send, and included only if you leave the "Include debug info" toggle on — with the app version, a general device/OS description (for example "Pixel 8 · Android 15"), and the game or screen you were on. It never includes your hand, an opponent's cards, or any other player's private information, and nothing is ever sent automatically.

Who can play Bonkey Cards

Bonkey Cards is a general-audience app (Games → Card category, rated Everyone / PEGI 3) — classic trick-taking and matching card games with no violence, no gambling or real-money wagering, and no chat or open communication features. Players can only interact through structured, turn-based game moves exchanged over a direct Bluetooth connection to a device they chose to connect to.

Purchases

Every game in Bonkey Cards — 20+ in all — is free and always will be. Bonkey Cards has no in-app purchases at launch. Future updates may add optional in-app purchases (for example, additional card game packs or cosmetic unlocks). Any future purchase is processed entirely by the app store you downloaded from (Apple App Store, Google Play, or the Amazon Appstore) — those stores handle payment, and Bonkey Apps never receives or stores your payment details or any personal data through that process. This section will be updated with specifics if and when any purchase is introduced.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any data to third parties, because we do not collect any data to share in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new "Last updated" date above.
